Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie follows Dom Cobb, a thief with the ability to enter peopl dreams and steal secrets from their subconscious. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he performs an almost impossible task: planting an idea in someone's mind, also known as "inception."
